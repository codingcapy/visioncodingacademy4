Vision Coding Summer Camp 2024

Explore exciting IT Technology and participate in fun and entertaining activities this summer!

Vision Coding Academy invites you to Summer 2024 Event where you will experience Virtual Reality using Oculus headsets and build hands-on Arduino electronic devices that you will program code for them yourself. Mixed with fun activities like kayaking, cycling and hiking scenic places like Grouse Mountain and Stanley Park, the Vision Coding Summer Camp will be an unforgettable experience that will make you more knowledgeable and passionate by the end of it.

Visit Microsoft Headquarters in Seattle

Ready to ignite your passion for technology and innovation? Embark on an unforgettable journey to the heart of tech brilliance – Microsoft Headquarters in Seattle. Walk the halls where groundbreaking software is born and meet the minds shaping the future of tech. Participate in interactive sessions, witness live demos, and dive into cutting-edge projects. Connect with industry leaders, top developers, and like-minded peers. This is your chance to build invaluable professional relationships. Get an insider’s view of the diverse roles and career paths within one of the world’s leading tech giants. Your dream job could be just a conversation away! Explore the latest advancements in AI, cloud computing, cybersecurity, and more. See firsthand how Microsoft is transforming the world. Go beyond the surface with in-depth tours of the Microsoft campus and its state-of-the-art facilities.
