Vision Coding Summer Camp 2024

Explore exciting IT Technology and participate in fun and entertaining activities this summer!

Vision Coding Academy invites you to Summer Camp 2024 Event where you will experience Virtual Reality using Oculus headsets and build hands-on Arduino electronic devices that you will program code for them yourself. Mixed with fun activities like kayaking, cycling and hiking scenic places like Grouse Mountain and Stanley Park, the Vision Coding Summer Camp will be an unforgettable experience that will make you more knowledgeable and passionate by the end of it.

Visit Microsoft Headquarters in Seattle

Ready to ignite your passion for technology and innovation? Embark on an unforgettable journey to the heart of tech brilliance – Microsoft Headquarters in Seattle. Walk the halls where groundbreaking software is born and meet the minds shaping the future of tech. Participate in interactive sessions, witness live demos, and dive into cutting-edge projects. Connect with industry leaders, top developers, and like-minded peers. Get an insider’s view of the diverse roles and career paths within one of the world’s leading tech giants. Explore the latest advancements in AI, cloud computing, cybersecurity. See firsthand how Microsoft is transforming the world. Go beyond the surface with in-depth tours of the Microsoft campus and its state-of-the-art facilities.

Trip to Banff

If you're seeking an unforgettable escape into the heart of breathtaking landscapes, look no further than Banff. Nestled in the Canadian Rockies, Banff is your gateway to awe-inspiring beauty and endless exploration. From towering mountain peaks to crystal-clear lakes, every view in Banff is postcard-perfect. Capture the beauty of iconic spots like Lake Louise and Moraine Lake. Whether you're into hiking, skiing, canoeing, or wildlife watching, Banff offers thrilling activities for every season and skill level.

Trip to Whistler

Discover Whistler, BC, the premier destination for those craving exhilarating experiences and stunning landscapes. Nestled in the majestic Coast Mountains, Whistler is your ticket to unforgettable moments and unparalleled beauty. Enjoy mountain biking, hiking, golfing, and zip-lining amidst lush forests and alpine meadows. From the peaks of Whistler and Blackcomb mountains to the tranquil waters of Lost Lake, every corner of Whistler offers postcard-worthy scenery. Explore the lively Whistler Village, packed with shops, restaurants, and entertainment.
